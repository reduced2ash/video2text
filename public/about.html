About VideoToText

VideoToText is a free, AI-powered tool that converts videos from YouTube, Instagram, and X (Twitter) into accurate text transcriptions. Built for creators, researchers, students, and anyone who needs to extract text from video content.

Our Mission

We believe that video content should be accessible to everyone. Whether you need transcripts for accessibility, content repurposing, studying, or research, VideoToText makes it simple and free to convert any video to text.

How We're Different

  • 100% Free – No hidden costs, no premium tiers, no limits on conversions
  • No Sign-Up Required – Start converting immediately, no account needed
  • Privacy-First – We don't store your videos or transcriptions
  • State-of-the-Art AI – Powered by Whisper, the most accurate speech recognition model
  • Multiple Platforms – YouTube, Instagram, and X all in one place

The Technology

VideoToText uses OpenAI's Whisper model through Groq's lightning-fast inference engine. This gives you accurate transcriptions in seconds, with support for over 50 languages and automatic language detection.

Who Uses VideoToText?

  • Content Creators – Repurpose video content into blog posts and articles
  • Students – Get transcripts of lectures and educational videos
  • Journalists – Quickly transcribe interviews and news clips
  • Researchers – Analyze video content in text format
  • Marketers – Extract quotes and insights from competitor content
